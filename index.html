Classical (CSE)

$$P_v = k\,f^{\alpha}\,B_{pk}^{\beta}$$

Modified (MSE)

$$P_v = k_h\,f^{\alpha}\,B_{pk}^{\beta} + k_e\,(f\,B_{pk})^2$$

$$P_v = \frac{k_i}{T}\int_0^T \left|\frac{dB}{dt}\right|^{\alpha} (\Delta B)^{\beta-\alpha}\,dt$$

$$ P_v = \frac{1}{T} \int_0^T \left( k_h |B(t)|^{\beta_h - \alpha} + k_e |B(t)|^{2-\alpha} \right) \left| \frac{dB}{dt} \right|^\alpha dt $$
